LÈON BREITLING
1884

Breitling is a Swiss luxury watch brand, founded in 1884 by Léon Breitling. The word “Breitling” itself is simply the founder’s family name, which later became the brand’s identity. Over time, the name has grown to symbolize precision, aviation, adventure, and luxury craftsmanship.
